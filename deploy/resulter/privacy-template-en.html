Privacy Policy

1. Controller

The controller within the meaning of the GDPR is the operator of this website named in the Legal Notice.

2. Data Processed and Purposes

2.1 Competition Results

This website displays results from orienteering competitions. The data shown (names, times, rankings) was publicly published by the respective event organisers. Competitors consent to the processing and publication of their result data when they register for a competition.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent given at competition registration) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in sports documentation).

2.2 User Login (Administration)

Access to the administration area is restricted to a small number of individually known users. They are personally informed about the processing of their data in advance and give their explicit consent before an account is created. Authentication is handled by Keycloak (a self-hosted identity provider). No user data is shared with third parties.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (explicit consent).

3. Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses only technically necessary cookies and browser storage:

  • Session cookie: Maintains the login session (administrators only). Deleted upon logout or when the browser is closed.
  • XSRF-TOKEN: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks.
  • sessionStorage: Temporarily stores data during the login process. Deleted when the browser tab is closed.
  • localStorage (userLocale): Stores the selected language preference (German/English).

No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are used. A cookie consent banner is therefore not required (§ 25(2)(2) TTDSG).

4. No Disclosure to Third Parties

Personal data is not disclosed to third parties. No external analytics, advertising, or social media services are integrated.

5. Hosting

This website is hosted on a dedicated server in Germany. When the website is accessed, the IP address and the date and time of the request are stored in the server log for technical reasons. This data is used solely to ensure reliable operation and is deleted after a maximum of 7 days.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure operation).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access (Art. 15 GDPR), rectification (Art. 16 GDPR), erasure (Art. 17 GDPR), restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR), and objection (Art. 21 GDPR). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection supervisory authority.
